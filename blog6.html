Now that we got that out of the way, I will shift my topic towards the Ronald Mcdonald house. I did not know that the McDonald house was so helpful and involved within the community.
Let's straight to the bread and butter. Who is the Ronald McDnald House of Charities? A charity organization who provides shelter and support for families with family members in the hospital. They basically provide housing and food for families who live within 30 miles radius minimum.
How are they funded? 15% of their funding comes from the Franchise (McDonalds) and approx. 85% from the community of people. I am very proud to say that, Tuconcians, yes, Tucsonians. Civilians who encompass Tucson, raise $1.1 Million annually!!! Woooow!
