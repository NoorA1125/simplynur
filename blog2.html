Poverty level is at an all time low and the government is not giving it the attention it deserves. SNAP and WIC cannot be used for diapers, nor can Food Stamps. This is still a big deal considering kids go through $936 worth of diapers in MONTH! Thats $100/month of a single child on diapers alone. With utilities taking up a majority of ones income (working minimum wage), $100 is A LOT. So much so, that it has reached a point where families have to decide between paying their bills for the month or buyinh diapers. It is a very unfortunate and very disheartening state to be mind.
Allah the Al-Mighty is so great, he's sent help for families in that state. This is WHY the Diaper Bank exists. They understand that something as little as diapers, an everyday necessity for both, the old and young can affect the functionality of a family (mental stress, fatigue etc.) as well as jobs (some day cares REQUIRE a child be brought in with diapers). The Diaper Banks works very hard of off volunteers to make sure diapers is the least of a families concern, so that they can focus on what really matters.
