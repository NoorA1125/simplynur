    Peace Corp - Blog 2

    By: Noor Abdikadir 29th February, 2016

    I chose to do my service hours for Project Focus. How I came to this decision was pretty simple, upfront, and unexpected. I remember telling the teacher I was a web developer. She asked me before class was dismissed if I had decided on what to focus my hours on and I still hadn't had a damn clue.

    So, she proposed me a offer I couldn't refuse; be the project focus's developer. I accepted without a question of doubt. Why you may ask? Simple. I want to
    1) Dust off my skillsets 2) It's something I am capable of doing with little to no effort 3) It's a important role that affects a large group
